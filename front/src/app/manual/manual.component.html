Welcome to Machine Witness

Basic Operations

Hold WASD to walk

Drag your mouse around while holding the left button to look around

Coding Problem Portals

The cube-like portals are generated by other players like you

Other portals are predefined by us

Walk into them to see what happens

Coding Problems

When you go inside a problem through a portal

You will see a problem interface

The left pannel contains some information

The middle panel contains your instructions

The right panel contains the input, the expected output, and the memory status

Define Your Own Problem

After you finish all the problems in a map

You may click the pencil button below and define your own problem
